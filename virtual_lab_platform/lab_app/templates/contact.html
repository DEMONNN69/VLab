Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our platform.

How quickly do you respond to emails?

As MCT RGIT Students ourselves, we typically respond within 24-48 hours during weekdays. For urgent technical issues, we aim to respond within 12 hours.

Can I suggest new experiments or features?

Absolutely! We welcome all suggestions from fellow MCT RGIT Students. Your feedback helps us improve the platform and add content that's actually useful for engineering education.

Do you provide academic support?

While we can't provide direct tutoring, we're happy to help with platform-related questions and can guide you to the right resources for your specific engineering topics.

Can MCT RGIT institution collaborate with you?

Yes! We're open to collaborating with MCT RGIT and other educational institutions to enhance the platform and make it more beneficial for students. Contact us to discuss partnership opportunities.

We're Here to Help!

As fellow MCT RGIT engineering students, we understand your challenges and are committed to helping you succeed. Don't hesitate to reach out with any questions or feedback.
